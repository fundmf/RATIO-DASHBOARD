BTC/MSTR Spread Divergence
This chart takes (BTC HIGH dist − MSTR HIGH dist) ÷ (BTC LOW dist − MSTR LOW dist), then divides by its own 20-day rolling volatility. This puts all time periods on equal footing — a move of the same size means the same thing whether markets are calm or chaotic. Above 0 = highs diverging relative to lows. Below 0 = lows diverging relative to highs. Magnitude is comparable across all periods.
